### Global and Env Variables 
Easily add, edit, and remove your environment variables and sync them to your pods.


### Secrets Management

KoalaOps is getting a brand new "Secrets" feature to simplify managing secret information across your environments!

- Easily add or remove secrets: Securely store and manage sensitive data like passwords, API keys, and more.
- Control access: Choose exactly which secrets and their associated keys are available in each environment, ensuring fine-grained control.
- Seamless syncing: Relax while KoalaOps automatically syncs the right secrets from your chosen secrets manager directly to your running pods, taking care of the heavy lifting.
